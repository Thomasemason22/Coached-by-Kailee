What is included

Coaching built around your life.

The program gives you structure without making your life smaller. Each piece is designed to keep progress measurable, realistic, and easy to understand.

Custom training plan

Workouts are matched to your goal, schedule, fitness level, and where you train.

Nutrition priorities

Guidance around protein, meals, habits, and macros when they make sense for your goal.

Weekly check-ins

Progress, adherence, wins, obstacles, and next adjustments are reviewed each week.

Direct accountability

Support from Kailee helps you stay consistent through busy weeks, travel, and stress.

Progress tracking

Training, habits, body feedback, and check-in notes help guide smart adjustments.

Flexible adjustments

Your plan can evolve as your schedule, recovery, confidence, and goals change.

How it works

The process is simple.

  • Apply with your goals, training history, lifestyle, and commitment level.
  • Kailee reviews your application and follows up within 48 hours.
  • If it is a fit, your plan is built around your starting point.
  • You check in weekly, adjust what needs adjusting, and keep moving.
